90-Day Strategic Blueprint

A phased approach to pivot from broad obscurity to local dominance.

  • Update Meta Data: Change Title Tags and H1s on core pages.
    From: "Executive Coaching for High Performance"
    To: "Executive Coaching in Vancouver | High Performance"
  • Google Business Profile: Claim and optimize to dominate the "Map Pack".
  • Quick Win: Intercept competitors (Kwela) by satisfying local intent.
  • Audit Blog: Identify weak/competing articles (e.g., 5 posts about "meetings").
  • The Merge: Combine weak posts into comprehensive Power Pages.
  • Technical Fix: Implement 301 Redirects to aggregate authority and clean up Google's index.
  • Local Digital PR: Target features in Business in Vancouver (BIV) or BCBusiness.
  • Topic: "The Crisis in Vancouver Corporate Culture" (Leveraging Greg's expertise).
  • Result: High-value local backlinks to boost Domain Rating (DR).
